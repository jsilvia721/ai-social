# Mission Statement Brainstorm

**Date:** 2026-03-14
**Status:** Final

## Mission Statement

> We build autonomous AI agents that make professional business capabilities accessible to small teams, starting with social media. Our first agent researches trends, creates content, publishes across platforms, and optimizes its own strategy — so small business owners can grow their audience while focusing on what they actually do.

## What We're Building

An autonomous AI agent platform, with social media management as the first vertical. The platform handles the full lifecycle that a human social media manager would: trend research, content strategy, content creation (text + media), cross-platform publishing, performance tracking, and self-optimization.

## Why This Framing

### Key Decisions

1. **Enablement over replacement** — The mission is about making professional capabilities *accessible* to small teams, not about replacing humans. This frames AI as an equalizer rather than a cost-cutting tool.

2. **Small business owners (1-5 people)** — The target user is someone who can't justify a dedicated social media hire but needs a consistent, quality social presence to grow their business.

3. **"Starting with social media"** — The platform vision is broader than one vertical. The underlying agent architecture (research → strategy → execution → measurement → optimization) is designed to be replicable across other business functions.

4. **Outcome-oriented** — The promise is *audience growth while you focus on your business*, not feature specs. This keeps the Brainstorm agent focused on user outcomes rather than technical novelty.

5. **Full-job framing** — The agent does the entire job (research, create, publish, optimize), not just scheduling. This sets a high bar and prevents the roadmap from settling for partial automation.

### How This Guides the Brainstorm Agent

When evaluating roadmap ideas, the Brainstorm agent should filter through:

- **Does this help small business owners grow their audience?** If not, deprioritize.
- **Does this reduce the need for human intervention?** The goal is autonomy with human oversight, not a better manual tool.
- **Does this expand what's accessible?** Features should bring professional-grade capabilities within reach, not add complexity.
- **Is this the full job?** Prefer features that close gaps in the end-to-end lifecycle over features that polish one part.
